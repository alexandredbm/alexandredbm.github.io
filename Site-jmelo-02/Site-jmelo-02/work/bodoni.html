Bodoni
Typeface specimen - 2021
As part of my studies, I've created a specimen for the Bodoni font family, in the form of a double-sided poster (50x70) printed in black ink.
For this project, I aimed to showcase the elegance and delicacy of Bodoni, capturing its beauty and versatility in a comprehensive manner. The specimen encompass various weights and styles, allowing for a thorough exploration of the distinctive features that make this iconic typeface so captivating.
