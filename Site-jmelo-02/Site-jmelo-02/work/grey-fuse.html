Grey Fuse
Editorial - 2023
Grey Fuse is a academic group project that delves into the environmental challenges faced by five countries, each of which is the focus of one of our Zines. Through extensive research, we have examined the impact of climate change on these countries and their people, as well as the efforts being made to mitigate these effects. Our goal is to provide a platform for sharing information and perspectives on these urgent issues, and to inspire action towards a more sustainable future.
The final output of our research is five Zines, each with its own unique take on the challenges faced by these countries. Together, they offer a comprehensive exploration of the complex and pressing issue of climate change in our world today.
