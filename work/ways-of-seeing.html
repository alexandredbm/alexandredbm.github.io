Ways of Seeing
Posters - 2022
"Ways of Seeing" is a documentary series conceived by John Berger and produced by Mike Dibb. The series was broadcasted by the BBC in 1972 and later adapted into a book with the same title.
In this academic project, the task was to create 7 posters that embody an experimental and procedural approach capable of invoking different dichotomies:

  • Hierarchy / anarchy
  • Construction / deconstruction
  • Centered / peripheral
  • Clustered / dispersed
  • Depth / surface
  • Legible / illegible
  • Positive / negative
